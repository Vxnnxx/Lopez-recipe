Pancit

Pancit is a staple Filipino dish found at numerous feasts and celebrations, consisting of stir-fried noodles with meat and vegetables such as chicken, pork, shrimp, celery, carrots, onions, garlic, and cabbage.

  • Ingredients

  • 1 Carrot, medium
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • 15 pieces Green beans
  • ½ Head cabbage, small
  • 1 Onion, medium
  • 1 Scallions or spring onions
  • 50 g Soy chunks, dry
  • 4 1/2 cups Vegetable broth
  • 1 Chili garlic sauce or other hot sauce
  • 4 tbsp Mushroom oyster sauce, vegetarian
  • 1 pack Flour stick noodles, dry
  • 1 Pepper, Ground
  • 3 tbsp Neutral oil
  • Sliced calamansi (Philippine Lime, lime, or lemon for squeezing)
  • 5 Shiitake mushrooms (fresh or rehydrated (sliced))
